My first attempt at making a complete functional website with Bootstrap.

During the beginning of 2020 most of us started working from home and while the whole world is going in to a shutdown, everyone now is stuck at home, so I decided to pick up a new skill set. I was always interested in Web Design, one to create a Web fashion portfolio and one for my small pet's product business. I used this time to take a online bootcamp for Full Stack development, starting a new journey to become a web developer.

Before my decision of joining a Bootcamp I've tried my a hand on making a fully working website for my small business. Teaching myself with W3School, other Web Design sites, and Youtube tutorials, I was able to make a somewhat function website with Bootstrap but with much to improve.
