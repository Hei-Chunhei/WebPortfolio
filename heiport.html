My Menswear Design Portfolio Website

Research

First, needed a website for my own fashion portfolio, I had plenty of content/ photos to add to this website. I needed a template, color that would fit my style, preferably something with a minimalistic feel.

Content

Landing page needed a direct explaination of what this website is. I wanted to set a dark serious mood, with intro, jpegs of my fashion sketches, full resume, at least the contact page.

  • Landing page, with buttons to go to each categories.
  • Intro: First button, intro page. Adding sketches to banner to be first thing you see.
  • Work: Portfolio sketches jpegs.
  • Resume: I've created my own resume with logo of companies. Added to the page to stand out.
  • Contact: Contact page to MailChimp (not active for this portfolio).
