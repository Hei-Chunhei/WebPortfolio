New and improved Furry Lift

Furry Lift, a pet's products accessories brand website.

Research

I needed a more product driven design for the new Furry Lift website. Needed to show case product better and more organized for the info and photos, and easier to navigate.

Content

Landing page showing the Furry Lift logo, then carousel showing detail shots of the product, linking to Amazon and detail page. Details explaining where products can be purchase, a foo section to add reviews and photos by customers.

  • Landing page, with navbar to go to each product to get in to detail page.
  • Carousel showing all the products, each card with detail shots and links to both Amazon and to detail page.
  • Clear line up of products.
  • Each products gets a detail page explaining info for each item.
